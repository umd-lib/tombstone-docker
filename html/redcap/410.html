UMD Libraries REDCap Retired

The UMD Libraries REDCap instance has been retired, and REDCap is now available to the UMD community via a partnership with University of Maryland, Baltimore. University of Maryland, College Park and University of Maryland, Baltimore form a single research entity that leverages strengths of both universities to advance interdisciplinary research and foster innovation. This relationship provides a foundation for the agreement between institutions to maintain a single REDCap service supported by the UMB Institute for Clinical and Translational Research (ICTR), and the UMB Center for Information Technology Services (CITS) that is available to all researchers at both universities.

The service includes access to a REDCap instance for research that does not involve medical system data, a REDCap instance built specifically to protect medical system data, and support from the ICTR REDCap admins. For more information, please visit Access and Use REDcap.
