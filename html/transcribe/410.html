The Transcribe Maryland Pilot Project has concluded.

Transcribe Maryland was a University of Maryland Libraries pilot project, available from 2016 to 2021, in which participants worked to improve access to our Special Collections documenting state of Maryland history and culture, and other fascinating topics. Users transcribed historic documents from a variety of collections that represented the personal, political, and cultural lives of a fascinating array of people.

While the pilot has ended, users can still refer to the following resources:

  • UMD Libraries Digital Collections
  • Transcribe Maryland in the Internet Archive
  • UMD Libraries Special Collections

For additional questions about the project, please contact UMD Libraries Special Collections.
